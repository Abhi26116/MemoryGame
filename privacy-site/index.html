Privacy Policy — Memory Match

Effective date: June 26, 2026

Memory Match (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a family-friendly game enjoyed by players of all ages, including children. We care deeply about your privacy. This policy explains what information is — and isn’t — collected when you use the App.

We do not collect personal information

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Advertising (third party)

The App shows advertisements through Google AdMob. Because the App may be used by children, ads are served in a child-directed, non-personalized (contextual) way. Ads are not based on a user’s interests or behavior, and the App does not allow ad tracking across apps (no IDFA / App Tracking Transparency prompt).

To deliver these ads and to prevent fraud and abuse, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may process limited technical information such as a device identifier, coarse device information, and basic app-interaction data (for example, how often an ad is shown). This is used only to serve and measure non-personalized ads, and is handled under Google’s policies. See Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not sell or share personal information, and we do not build user profiles or use data for behavioral advertising.

Purchases

The App offers an optional, one-time “Remove Ads” in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store; we do not receive or store your payment information. A parental gate is shown before any purchase can be made.

Children’s privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

The App is designed to comply with the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising is limited to non-personalized, age-appropriate (G-rated) content, and no behavioral tracking is performed.

Data retention & security

We do not retain personal information because we do not collect it. Any limited technical data processed by Google for ad delivery is retained according to Google’s policies.

Parental choices

You can remove ads entirely via the optional in-app purchase. You can also reset or limit ad-related identifiers in your device settings (Settings → Privacy & Security).

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
